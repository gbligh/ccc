This annual credit must be booked through Amex Travel or by calling Platinum Card Travel Service. If you book a refundable hotel with this credit you can cancel the reservation after the charge has posted for a refund. That's $200 cash back!

Nexus lets you skip the security and customs lines at Canadian airports and land border crossings. It costs $50 USD for 5 years and is worth getting if you travel often. Information on applying can be found at CBSA's website

Your card gives you Hilton Honours Gold Status which provices space-available room upgrades and free breakfast. Marriott Bonvoy Gold Elite Status which provices space-available room upgrades and late check-out and Radisson Rewards Americas Gold Status which provices space-available room upgrades and early check-in/late check-out.

Get access to the Amex priority security lane at Toronto. You can register and get your QR code for access here. You can also get the valet fee waived when paying for parking at Terminal 1 by presenting your Amex Platinum Card.

Your card gives you and one guest complementary access to many lounges by simply showing the card. You can search through the available lounges with Amex's Lounge Search Tool. Some lounges require Priority Pass registration, see below for more.

Priority Pass is a separate complementary airport lounge access pass that you need to register for. It gives you access to different lounges than your Amex. To see what pass you need check the access notes in the available lounges with Amex's Lounge Search Tool. To register call the number on the back of your card and ask to register for priority pass. The physical pass can take 2-3 months to arrive. Pro Tip: ask the customer service rep to call you back with your pass number. You can download the priority pass app on your phone to access these lounges without the physical card.

On your Amex mobile app or website dashboard navigate to the "Amex Offers" section to see any available discounts. Click "Register to Card" to activate the discount and then make a purchase from the relevant store in the amount required to receive the discount. The discount will be automatically applied to your Amex statement.
